Brief Bio of Doctor Carlos

I am Carlos C. Gomez, PhD, a licensed psychologist with clinical experience since 1998 and licensure obtained in 2006. With over 16 years in private practice and academia, my expertise spans psychology, psychometrics, and data science. I have a strong foundation in analytical skills and exploratory data analysis and am currently transitioning into the tech field. I am open to professional opportunities and collaborations that leverage technology to improve healthcare and promote comprehensive wellness for individuals, patients, providers, and delivery systems.
